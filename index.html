Innovating Affordable, High-Performance Shake Tables for Education and Research

About Us

Seismove is dedicated to designing and manufacturing shake tables for seismic education and research. Our goal is to provide reliable and innovative tools for students and researchers to simulate and study seismic events.

Our Products

Precursor

Precursor is our entry-level, affordable shake table designed to deliver exceptional performance without compromise. Learn more about its features and capabilities on our GitHub page.

Mainshock

Mainshock is our flagship shake table that offers advanced features and unparalleled performance. Mainshock is under development and will be available soon. Stay tuned for updates!

Tandem

Tandem is our dual-axis shake table that provides bi-directional shaking for simulating complex seismic events. Tandem is under development and will be available soon. Stay tuned for updates!
