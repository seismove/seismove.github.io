About Us

Seismove is dedicated to designing and manufacturing shake tables for seismic education and research. Our mission is to bring reliable, high-performance seismic simulation devices to classrooms and labs around the world at an affordable cost.

With a team of engineers and educators, we focus on continuous innovation, ensuring our shake tables meet the evolving needs of students, researchers, and professional engineers alike.
